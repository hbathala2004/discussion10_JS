Los Angeles

Location: Los Angeles, California Country: United States of America Geographical Location: North America.

About: Los Angeles is a sprawling Southern California city and the center of the nation’s film and television industry. Near its iconic Hollywood sign, studios such as Paramount Pictures, Universal, and Warner Brothers offer behind-the-scenes tours. On Hollywood Boulevard, TCL Chinese Theatre displays celebrities’ hand- and footprints, the Walk of Fame honors thousands of luminaries, and vendors sell maps to stars’ homes.

Climate: Los Angeles is famous for its idyllic, warm climate. Temperatures are high but pleasant all year round, while rainfall is uncommon and mainly limited to the winter months.

Chiang Mai

Location: Chiang Mai, Thailand Country: Thailand Geographical Location: Asia.

About: Chiang Mai, nestled in the lush hills of northern Thailand, is a city that captivates with its unique blend of cultural richness and natural beauty. Situated approximately 700 kilometers north of Bangkok, Chiang Mai is the largest city in the region and serves as the capital of the province of the same name. Its geographical location is nothing short of enchanting, surrounded by forested mountains and picturesque landscapes.

Climate: Chiang Mai, Thailand, experiences a tropical climate with distinct seasons. The cool season, from November to February, is the most pleasant for visitors, featuring comfortable temperatures ranging from 15 to 30 degrees Celsius. The hot season, lasting from March to June, sees high temperatures exceeding 30 degrees Celsius, requiring precautions against the heat.

Milan

Location: Milan, Italy Country: Italy Geographical Location: Europe.

About: Milan is the capital city of the region of Lombardy in northern Italy. It is the second largest city by population in Italy, behind Rome. It is Italy's leading financial center and its most prosperous manufacturing and commercial city.

Climate: Milan features a mid-latitude, four-season humid subtropical climate. Milan's climate is similar to much of Northern Italy's inland plains, with hot, humid summers and cold, foggy winters.

Paris

Location: Paris, France Country: France Geographical Location: Europe.

About: Paris is the capital and most populous city of France. Situated on the Seine River, in the north of the country, it is in the center of the Île-de-France region, also known as the région parisienne, "Paris Region".

Climate: The winters are generally cold but not freezing, and the summers can be hot, although only occasionally. The rain is frequent.

Rome

Location: Rome, Italy Country: Italy Geographical Location: Europe.

About: Rome, the Eternal City, is a priceless treasure trove of history, culture, and architectural beauty. With its iconic monuments, bustling squares, and ancient remains, the Italian capital offers an unforgettable experience for travelers from all over the world.

Climate: Rome has a Mediterranean climate with cool winters and warm and hot summers.
